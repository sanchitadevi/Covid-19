Wash your hand frequently atleast for 20sec. Use hand-sanitizer to keep your hand germs free.

Don't forget to use mask. Must know how to use it and dispose it after used.

Wear gloves while grocery shopping or during work.

Avoid touching your eyes ,nose and mouth without washing your hand.

Avoid public transports. If possible use cycle ,car or walk.

Should avoid public gathering or crowdy place. Maintain distance yourself from others.

If you're tested Covid-positive then keep yourself isolated atleast for 14 days.
